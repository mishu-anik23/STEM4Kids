## ðŸ† PROGRESSION SYSTEM

### Stars & Scoring
- **3 Stars**: 90-100% correct, fast completion
- **2 Stars**: 70-89% correct
- **1 Star**: 50-69% correct
- **Try Again**: Below 50%

### Rewards
- **Coins per level**: 10 (1 star), 20 (2 stars), 30 (3 stars)
- **Bonus coins**: Daily login, streak bonuses
- **Unlockables**: Avatar items, pet companions

### Leaderboard Categories
1. **Global Leaderboard**: Total stars
2. **Weekly Challenge**: Specific topic scores
3. **Friend Leaderboard**: Compare with classmates
4. **World Rankings**: Separate for each world

### Achievements (Badges)
- **Quick Thinker**: Complete 5 levels in a row with 3 stars
- **Persistent Learner**: Retry same level 3 times and succeed
- **Math Master**: Complete Math Island with all 3 stars
- **Science Whiz**: Complete all 4 worlds
- **Daily Dedicated**: 7-day streak
- **Helping Hand**: Complete all tutorial hints

## ðŸ“Š DIFFICULTY PROGRESSION

### Adaptive Learning
- **Success Rate > 80%**: Slightly increase difficulty
- **Success Rate < 50%**: Offer hint system, reduce difficulty
- **Time-based**: Faster completion = bonus points

### Hint System
- **Hint 1**: Visual clue (free)
- **Hint 2**: Partial answer (costs 5 coins)
- **Hint 3**: Full explanation (costs 10 coins)

### Accessibility Features
- **Text-to-Speech**: Read all questions
- **Adjustable Time**: No time pressure option
- **Color-blind Mode**: Pattern variations
- **Multiple Languages**: Support for 5+ languages
